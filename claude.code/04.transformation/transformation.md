# 25 Wealth & Personal Finance Transformation Products

A "transformation product" moves a client from **Point A (Pain)** to **Point B (Desired Result)**. These ideas are structured as compelling hooks designed for high-ticket drop-servicing, digital products, or coaching.

1. **The 700 Club Sprint**: From "Denied" to "Approved" — Repair your credit score by 100+ points in 90 days or less.
2. **The Debt Demolisher**: Liquidate $50k in high-interest consumer debt in 18 months without living on ramen.
3. **The Credit Line Launchpad**: Unlock $50k–$150k in 0% interest business credit to fund your next acquisition.
4. **The Student Loan Liquidation**: A strategic roadmap to settle or discharge student debt for pennies on the dollar.
5. **The Credit Repair Agency-in-a-Box**: Start a profitable drop-servicing credit business in 30 days using AI-driven automation.
