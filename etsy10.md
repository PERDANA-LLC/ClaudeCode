## Phase 4 — Get your first sales
*Couples are a specific audience — these channels work best*

### Step 16 — Turn on Etsy Ads at $1–3/day
Go to: Shop Manager → Marketing → Etsy Ads

Run for a minimum of 2 weeks to get keyword data. At $47, you only need one sale per $14 in ad spend to break even — most listings achieve this within the first week.

> **Tip:** Your first 5 reviews are worth more than any ad. Use ads to get those reviews fast, then organic search compounds from there.

### Step 17 — Share in 3 high-intent places today

1. **Reddit** — post in r/personalfinance, r/marriage, or r/relationships as a resource (not an ad). The "therapy is $200/hour" angle performs very well in these communities.
2. **Pinterest** — create a pin with your cover image. Couples' finance content has long shelf life on Pinterest compared to other platforms.
3. **Facebook groups** — search "married couples money" or "couples finances" for active groups with 10k+ members.

### Step 18 — Ask your first 3 buyers for a review

Send a follow-up message 3–4 days after purchase:

> "Hi! Hope the kit has already sparked a useful conversation. If you have 30 seconds, leaving a review helps other couples find it — and it genuinely makes a difference to a small shop."

> **Tip:** Couples who buy this together are twice as likely to leave a review — they share the experience and one of them usually feels motivated to tell others. Mention that in your follow-up.

### Step 19 — Create 2–3 duplicate listings with different angles
Same product, different titles and tags to capture more search terms:

- "Newlywed Financial Planner"
- "Couples Money Date Night Kit"
- "Marriage Budget Workbook for Two"

Each listing costs $0.20 to publish and gets its own independent search ranking.

### Step 20 — Check stats after 7 days and diagnose

Go to: Shop Manager → Stats

| Metric | If low → fix this |
|--------|-------------------|
| Views | Tags and title (not being found) |
| Click-through rate | Cover image (not compelling enough) |
| Conversion rate | Description or add the therapy comparison image |

> **Note:** At $47, conversion rate matters more than traffic volume. A listing with 100 views and 5 sales outperforms one with 1,000 views and 3 sales.
